About SpendSmart

SpendSmart was born from my personal journey with managing pocket money. I often found myself wishing I had a guardian for my finances, someone to watch over my spending and alert me before I overspent on the money i had. I wanted a tool that could help me allocate my money wisely across different expenses—whether it was for entertainment, food, or essential school supplies like books and stationery and monitor how i used money on them. One of my challenges was understanding the financial impact of eating out versus cooking at home. To solve this, I started had to devise a way tracking my spending: one week dedicated to dining out and another to cooking meals then compare costs and make informed decisions about where my money was going. Beyond personal finance, SpendSmart was also inspired by a desire to help others facing similar challenges. I envisioned a tool that not only tracks expenses but also guides users toward financial awareness and smart spending habits. SpendSmart is not just about managing money—it's about empowering users to take control of their finances and achieve their financial goals
